Our Menu

Chicken

Pair it with our house-made sauces for a perfect match. Customers especially love the lemon herb marinade and honey garlic glaze. A must-try!

Beef

Tender slices of beef slow-cooked in a rich and savory sauce. A hearty dish that's full of flavor and cooked to perfection.
Each bite melts in your mouth, bursting with deep umami and spice. Our chefs use traditional slow-braising methods to lock in taste.
Popular options include spicy chili beef, garlic soy beef tips, and our chef's special red wine reduction.

Sushi

A delightful selection of fresh sushi rolls made with premium ingredients. Includes tuna, salmon, avocado, and more. A favorite for seafood lovers!
Our sushi chefs prepare each roll with precision and care. Enjoy classic rolls like California and Spicy Tuna, or try something bold like our Dragon Roll.
We also offer vegetarian options and special seasonal creations with mango, eel, or seared scallops.
